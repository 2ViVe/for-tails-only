Jeff's Story

This experience has been like an emotional rollercoaster, because a few weeks after I was introduced to ZoiVi I was involved in a really bad accident. I was driving home at night when I hit a deep pothole on the highway that threw me headfirst into a stone barrier. I never knew what hit me, only that when I regained consciousness in the hospital I was told that the crash had cracked my jaw in 3 sections, and my nerves had been severed. I was told that I would not be able to eat food, or chew anything for a month, actually entire time I was in the hospital I was on I.V. drips for so "so called" food.

I remember when some of the reps from ZoiVi came to see me, and the company had sent some products with them. They whipped up some shakes and I can say pretty much nursed me back to health.

According to the doctors, I was not supposed to be able to do anything with my jaw for about 2 months, I was going to need metal clips and rods to recuperate, and because of the time it was going to take to heal I would have to undergo physical therapy to be able to use my jaw again.

I don't know if it was only ZoiVi products that did it, but I was out of the hospital before 2 weeks were up, I was eating food regularly by my third week, and although I have 3 titanium plates in my jaw, I didn't need the rods or wires. I decided to go full swing with ZoiVi and the products, and since then never looked back. I had gained a lot of weight throughout the years, and it was time for me to re-invent myself. I started with the DTX and Burn, and lost 10 lbs in my first two weeks. Then I added the Enzymes to my eating habits and watched the weight literally fall off. Within 4 months I hit my weight goal of 38 lbs lost. Today, I just maintain my lifestyle, I am in the greatest shape of my life, and it feels great!

People always say things like "I wish it were easy, or one day I will get on it and try it" and sometimes they never really get to live the life they want to live because there is always an excuse stopping them. For me, that excuse was cut out of my life along with the nerves that were severed in my jaw. ZoiVi has allowed me to find myself, along with a sense of direction to be able to go out there and help people, through health and with that their finances. If you are considering ZoiVi, I would say DO IT! Just give yourself a chance, and see what it can do for you! For me, it gave me my life back, from health to understanding, I feel more complete today than I have ever felt in my entire life! Thank you ZoiVi, and the leadership of this company for being there for me!
