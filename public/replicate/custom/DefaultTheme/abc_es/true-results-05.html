Xiao's Story

Growing up, my weight was never too much of a problem. I was just looking to tone up, and when my friends asked me if I wanted to see something cool, I said yes. That's how I found out about ZoiVi. I couldn't' wait to get my beach body! However, I had one major problem with the products; the products were all natural, and because they were made from plants, fruits, roots, etc I was worried. I'm severely allergic to nearly all fruits, soybeans, nuts, tree nuts, shellfish, pollen, hair chemicals, dust, dairy, wheat, corn, sesame seeds, walnuts, scallop, water, sun, and MORE. When I had my allergen exam done, they found I was allergic to 84% of things in the world.

My allergies came about around the age of 10, and they got more and more severe as I grew older. My doctors told me that my body was just going through changes and there was nothing I can do to get rid of my allergies. I took allergy medicine for the past decade almost every single day and I even became immune to its medicinal benefits.

I had the opportunity to attend an event where I met the founder of the company. When I explained my situation to him, he told me that I would not be allergic to ZoiVi products because the products are so pure and natural. All they do is help bring your body back into balance by cleansing any toxic buildup.

He suggested that what I was really allergic to were the pesticides, chemicals, and hormones in everything I've been eating, and actually suggested that I use the DTX to detoxify my body and then take an EMZ and eat it with something I know I am slightly allergic to, and see what happens.

When I used the DTX the first time, 3 pounds of stuff come out! I would never have guessed I had that much toxic waste. Then I started using the Burn and EMZ. I was so focused on getting my beach body I almost forgot what the founder of the company had asked me to try until 2 weeks into the program. I was at a salad bar, picking my usual items, and it was then that I remembered what the founder had said and decided to add apples to my salad.

That day, was the first time in a decade that I was able to eat a full apple, completely, without ANY sort of allergic reaction. And since then I've been on a fruit craze and then anti-allergy craze where I tried to eat something I was allergic to before and see what would happen. Six months into the program now, I am still eliminating things from my allergy list. As for my beach body, two months into the regime, I had lost an inch off my waist. But what I am most happy about is that I can finally eat like a normal person again. Thank you ZoiVi for such wonderful products!
