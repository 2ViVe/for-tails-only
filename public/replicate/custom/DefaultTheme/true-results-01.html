Deniz's Story

If you had met me a couple of years ago you would not think it's the same me now. When I was younger I was very fit, I loved to play sports, and was very active. Eventually, I had less time to commit to these activities since I had to work and take care of my family. That's where my health started to decline. About 4 years ago I fell into a really bad depression and I would eat to make myself feel better. I saw my body changing, gaining a pound here and there. I would tell myself its not that bad, I would hide my weight gain by buying bigger clothes, but the bigger the clothes the bigger I got.

One night a group of friends invited me out, and I was shocked when I realized I could no longer force myself into a size 14. I looked in the mirror and broke down into tears, I couldn't believe the reflection I was looking at was me! I said to myself over and over "I'm so fat, I'm so fat!" I was so ashamed of my appearance that I avoided everyone. I started trying different things to lose the weight, but nothing changed, and it seemed that my weight gain was only getting worse. I was at my lowest point when my friend introduced me to ZoiVi and the products. After hearing the stories of everyone else that had used them, I was excited but nervous at the same time. I didn't want to be disappointed again. But I figured if a guarantee was confident enough to guarantee a product to work in two days, then I had nothing to lose by trying it out.

After using everything else out there I promised myself this would be the last time and it was! I started with the weight management set and lost 8 lbs within my first week! I couldn't believe it myself, and if my friend had not told me to take my before picture I still might not believe it today!

ZoiVi has changed my life! I didn't think that was even possible. After two weeks I lost 15lbs and continue to loose weight. By the end of my second month I was at 26 lbs lost, and am getting closer to my weight goal of 125lbs. Within a few months I was down to 174 lbs, the smallest I have ever been in years, and I dropped 6 sizes in clothing!! Shopping for new clothes never felt so good, and I am so excited to see my final result thanks to ZoiVi. Anyone who thought they could not do it now has a chance. Because if I did it, anyone can!
