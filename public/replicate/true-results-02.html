Deniz's Story

At some point in life everyone starts to take notice of their health, and their way of living. Throughout the years of having a son, working, and going through a divorce health became the last thing I was focused on. I was not aware of how I had let myself go until I woke up one day, looked in the mirror and realized I wasn't excited about I saw. I am 5'1'' and being any bit overweight shows easily on my small body. I started thinking of how growing up I was always thin, too thin actually. My family would tell me to eat more, and no matter what I ate, I was still skinny enough that my shoulder bones can be seen.Then I had my son and gained too much weight, baby fat that I just could not get rid of. As I started working again, I was stressed out and that added to the problem. Soon, I lost confidence in myself, and I could feel my body growing weak and tired. I forgot about my health, until I was accidentally introduced to ZoiVi.

When I found out about ZoiVi, and the results people were having with the products I was excited and nervous. My hopes that I found something that worked grew, but I was nervous that it wouldn't work for me. I had tried so many other "solutions" only to find out they were gimmicks and were not longterm."

ZoiVi had found me at a crucial time in my life, I was ready for change, and although I was skeptical I realized if I didn't at least try I would always think about the what ifs. Since I met people who had been able to get some pretty good results, I figured why not me.

I started with the DTX, since it was supposed to cleanse my body and make it easier to experience a weight-loss effect, replaced 1 meal a day with the shakes, and in my first week I lost about 9 lbs! I was SO excited!!! After the DTX, I would take 1 Burn along with my ZoiVi multi-vitamins, and noticed my skin began to clear up. I started to feel more energetic, and felt like I could even have enough energy to not only take care of my 7 year old son Ginga, but also workout. I wanted to see if I could get the "washboard" abs everyone talks about. My results by the end of my first month, going into 3 months, and then 4 months later I feel like my WHOLE LIFE has been changed. Today, I feel great about myself, even though I don't have a 6-pack, I realize my lifestyle, my energy, my confidence has completely been affected. I feel young again, 30 years old going on 21, and I can keep up with my 7 year old son Ginga!

I finally realized that ZoiVi is really an amazing experience. It is truly a lifestyle that anyone can work with. ZoiVi gave me my life back, and today many of my own friends have become a part of the lifestyle. I love ZoiVi!!
