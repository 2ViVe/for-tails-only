Emily's Story

I always heard about the freshman 15 people experience when they go off to college. After my first year, I thought I had gotten lucky, since I stayed the same weight and size. My sophomore year is where the problem began, my freshmen 15 didn't hit me until my 2nd year in college. I gained so much weight that I looked like a ball. My face was round, my stomach had never been so big before. My mom gave me the nickname fat girl, in chinese pronounced "FAY LOY". Maybe she thought it was cute, I certainly didn't. I didn't know what to do, I felt disgusting and heavy...

My childhood friend introduced me to ZoiVi. I never believed in weight-loss products, because I knew most had side effects, so I would always try to do things "naturally". But when I heard that the products are all natural, and I saw the results that many people had achieved, I was instantly interested. My biggest concern was to lose my growing beer belly. With these products, I started with the DTX, which is the productthat has shown me my flat stomach, that I've never seen before.

After using the DTX, I lost 3lbs of toxins, and got rid of my beer belly. My stomach has never looked so flat, in so many years, even my entire family was questioning me on what I was doing. I started using the BURN and EMZ, after I detoxed. I completely stopped consuming processed/junk food and started eating healthy and in smaller portions. In the beginning, I didn't work out at all, I still managed to lose 8 lbs in first 2 months without doing anything different in my day.

After that, I started to work out and tone up. Never before, would I imagine myself being in this situation. Not only have I lost weight, gained muscle mass, my skin has cleared up! The only thing I would have to worry about would be my allergies, I don't have to worry about acne or acne scars anymore. Many people that haven't seen me in a long time, didn't even recognize me. They said that I look completely different, my skin glows, and I look great!

My mom used to call me fat, and now she says "I can't call you fat anymore, I have to call you beautiful."
